The History of Harbane SMP

Brief Timeline of Harbane SMP

  • Harbane SMP was founded on the 27th of July.
  • It consists of 3 seasons Harbane 1, 2 and 3, each new one starts every new update.
  • Harbane 1 ended on 4th of August and Harbane 2 started on the 16th August.
  • Harbane 2 ended on 26th of November and Harbane 3 started on the 12th January.
  • Harbane 3 ended on 3rd of July and Harbane 4 started on the 7th of July.

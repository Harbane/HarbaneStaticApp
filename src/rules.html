Rules

Read these rules carefully before playing on Harbane smp

    Section A:
  1. No player under the age of 11 can play on the smp
  2. Players are not allowed to go in others account to join smp unless they have permission from the person who is actively using that account
  3. Players are not allowed to use cheats, mods or any other forms of hacks
  4. Players are not allowed to grief or steal in anyway
  5. Players are not allowed to threaten other players
  6. Players are not allowed to start war or a rebellion on smp or against smp
  7. Players are not allowed to get revenge on one another
  8. Trolling on the smp is not allowed
  9. Follow all the server rules
  10. You cant take, break or use anyone else property unless u have their permission
  11. Most dupe glitches are not allowed to see which ones are allowed, ask admin
    Section B:
  1. Server Commands will not be taken lightly and will only be done for emergency or anything else that's important
  2. If you do something that is not on the rules and you are not allowed to do it, then the rules will be updated and you will be warned, ban can not take place if it doesn't state in the rules, unless it is something serious that the player could of used common knowledge to avoid
  3. Admin have the rights to stop you from playing on the smp and only let you play when in supervising of admin (this will only happen if the admin is suspicious of said player and needs to be supervised)
  4. Admin have rights to be watching you if they deem you suspicious
  5. Players can only be teleported in extreme cases and it must come at a cost of one of their tools or armor
  6. If tp is required the exceptions can be made by admin if needed
  7. Bans will be taken very seriously
  8. Players may be temporarily banned for investigation if needed
  9. Netherite cannot be used as currency
  10. If a player steals or griefs then the victim of this action will get a portion of the persons valuable and that person will also be temporarily banned
  11. If you believe a player has broken one of these rules inform an admin and try to getter as much proof as possible such as screenshots, clips, and logs
    Section C:
  1. The active SMP seed can not be disclosed
  2. These rules can be changed any time and u will be notified of the change
  3. Do not take what happens on smp to real life
  4. If a player abuses their console perms then it will be immediately taken away from them
  5. By playing on SMP you automatically agree to the rules

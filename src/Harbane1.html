Harbane 1

What is Harbane 1

Harbane 1 was the first SMP, and it also happened to be the shortest. Despite the fact that Harbane 1 lasted only 12 days, we were able to obtain full netherite, create numerous farms and ultimately beat the game. Nonetheless, Harbane 1 was operated by a man (for the sake of keeping his name out of the public domain, we'll call him Justin Bendo) who was notorious for abusing his privellages and at times even hacked, He held a decent role in the building of Harbane SMP and also had a major role in its destruction. In Harbane 1, he used operator commands for his own benefit, causing the SMP to collapse and, to put it mildly, being "deposed from his throne". The members of Harbane 1 are the following people:

  • Haroon Ahmad (PhantomSlayer_)
  • Ahhnaaf Zaman (ESmolen)
  • Rayyan Saeed (Psyhole)
  • Humayl Siddiqui (Exterm1nat0r_)
  • Justin Bendo (polido)
